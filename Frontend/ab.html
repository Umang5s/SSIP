Overview

The full form of ITI is Industrial Training Institute and it is a government training organization responsible for providing high school students with industry-related education. At the same time, some trades can still be applied after the 8th grade. Specifically, these institutes are set up to offer technical information to students who have just passed the 10th level and are interested in gaining some technical knowledge rather than higher education. ITI is established by the Directorate-General for Employment and Training(DGET), the Ministry of skills development and entrepreneurship and the government of the union to provide training in different trades.

Vision

  • Our vision is to develop quality and industry employable human resource through vocational training for a sustainable economic and social advancement of the state.
  • The main goal of ITI is to train its candidates for the industry, to prepare them to be ready for work. ITIs also administer apprenticeship courses to make this possible.

Mission
  • To enhance the quality of vocational training in Gujarat.
  • To create strong interfaces and engagements with Industry for demand driven skilling and better employ ability of skilled human resource.
  • To create alignment with the national level skill initiatives for standardization of the course curriculum.
  • To strengthen the existing skill ecosystem through monitoring and bench marking.
